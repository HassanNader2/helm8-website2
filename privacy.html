Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Helm8 is a business automation consultancy operating in the United Arab Emirates, trading as Hassan Nader. Our website is helm8.co. For privacy matters, contact us at hello@helm8.co.

What data we collect

We collect personal data only when you choose to provide it. This includes:

  • Your name and business email address when you submit the contact form or free audit tool.
  • Your company name and a description of the business challenge you provide in the form.
  • Audit responses you complete in the free operations assessment tool.
  • Booking details when you schedule a call through our calendar (processed by Cal.com).

We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We do not run analytics that identify individual visitors.

How we use your data

Data you submit is used solely to respond to your enquiry or to follow up on an assessment you have requested. We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties. We do not add you to any mailing list without explicit consent.

Third-party processors

We use the following services to process form submissions and bookings on our behalf:

  • Formspree — receives contact form submissions and forwards them to us. Data is stored on Formspree servers in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • Cal.com — manages calendar bookings. When you book a call, your name, email, and selected time are processed by Cal.com in accordance with their privacy policy.

We do not control how these processors store or handle data beyond what their respective policies describe.

Data retention

Enquiry data is retained for as long as is necessary to complete the relevant sales or service conversation, and no longer than 24 months. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights under UAE PDPL

Under Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 (UAE Personal Data Protection Law), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@helm8.co. We will respond within 30 days.
